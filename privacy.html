Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

Kiko TikTok Bot ("the Service") is a personal automation tool operated by AI'm Not a Robot. This policy describes how we handle data in connection with the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Data Collection

The Service does not collect, store, or process personal data from any third-party users. It is used exclusively by its owner to publish video content to a single TikTok account.

3. TikTok API Data

The Service accesses TikTok account data solely for the purpose of uploading and publishing video content. Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's device and are never transmitted to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained only as long as necessary for the Service to function. They can be revoked at any time through TikTok's account settings.

6. Security

Access credentials are stored securely on a private, password-protected device. The Service runs locally and does not expose any public endpoints.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: kiko@aimnotarobot.com
